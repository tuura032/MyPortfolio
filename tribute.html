Elon Musk

The man who is making his own future
Musk, a co-founder of Tesla, shows off the world's "coolest" mass-market electric vehicle.

    Here's a timeline of Musk's major accomplishments:

  • 1971 - Musk was born June 28, 1971 Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1981 - At age 10, Musk taught himself computer programming.
  • 1988 - At the age of 17, Musk moved to Canada and pursued degrees in economics and physics. He later began a Ph. D. in applied physics but later dropped out to pursue an entreprenurial career.
  • 1995 - His first entrepreunrial venture was co-founding 'Zip2', a web software company.
  • 1999 - Next up was "x.com" which later became PayPal. eBay purchased PayPal in 2002 for 1.5 billion.
  • 2002 - Using his money from PayPal Elon founded SpaceX, an aerospace manufacturer and space transport services compnay, in order to make humans a multi-planetary species. He also invested in Tesla, Inc and became the CEO and product architect.
  • 2012 - Tesla launches the Model S line of electric cars. The Model S featured an electic 335 mile range, higher than any other electric car at the time. the Model S has won many awards, including awards for car of the year, safest car, and most environmental.
  • 2015 - On December 22, 2015, SpaceX made a huge breakthrough in the space industry by successfully landing the first stage of it's Falcon rocket. Prooving that rockets can be re-used was a huge step in reducing the cost of launching into space. As of 2018, 20 of 42 first stage Falcon 9 boosters have been recovered.
  • 2016 - Tesla acquired Solar City, a solar manufacturer that Musk inspired and invested in back in 2006.
  • 2016 - The massive success of the Model S and popularity of Tesla spurned the next generation of electric car, the Model 3. Notably, this model would feature an impressive 310 mile range (220 miles in standard) and a targeted price point of $35,000.
  • 2022 - Elon Musk is targeting a mission to Mars this year, however most believe his timeline is a bit optimistic.
